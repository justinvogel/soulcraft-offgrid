What We Build

Complete Off-Grid Intelligence

Nine integrated systems that keep your property smart, secure, and fully independent — no cloud, no subscription, no single point of failure.

Off-Grid Surveillance

Defend Cell Cam and Defend 360 — 4G cellular cameras, solar powered, no Wi-Fi required. Night vision, weather-resistant housings, and plans starting at $5/month.

Satellite & Backup Comms

Starlink as primary connectivity, cellular failover, and backup radio. Three layers of communication so you're never truly disconnected.

EMP Protection

Faraday cage enclosures, EMP-hardened backup inverters, and protected spare controllers. Your critical electronics survive what others won't.

Generator & Power

Solar arrays, battery storage, backup generators, and automatic transfer switches. Engineered to run your entire smart home indefinitely — grid or no grid.

Homestead Monitoring

Soil moisture, water quality, weather stations, propane tank levels, creek flood sensors, livestock monitoring, chicken coop automation, greenhouse controls — all on LoRaWAN feeding into your local AI.

Local AI Home Computer

Mac Mini or Mac Studio running local AI. Controls lighting, security, and automation without internet. Apple Silicon processes everything on-device — your data never leaves the property.

Mesh Networking

Property-to-property mesh networking on a LoRa radio backbone. Coordinate with neighbors, share alerts, and communicate — no internet required.

Perimeter Security

Motion sensors, driveway alerts, thermal cameras, and wildlife detection. Every event processed locally on your AI computer — no cloud, no latency, no exposure.

Drone Infrastructure

Autonomous drone delivery between neighboring properties. Share tools, seeds, medications, and parts via mesh network-coordinated flights. Landing pads, charging stations, and flight path management included.

Our Philosophy

Built on Three Principles

Every system we design honors the same foundational commitments.

01

Independence

Your home should function perfectly whether the grid is up or down, whether the internet is on or off. True autonomy means zero external dependencies.

02

Intelligence

Off-grid doesn't mean off-tech. Local AI brings the smartest automation available — processing on your property, learning your patterns, adapting in real time.

03

Resilience

Every system has redundancy. Every connection has a fallback. When one layer goes down, the next takes over seamlessly. Your home endures whatever comes.
